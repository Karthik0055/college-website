Pendurthi Government Polytechnic

                   

                        This college is located in near Pulgavanipalem road, In Pendurthi.
                        This offers a vast number of labs and good quality of education for all the students equally.
                        It offers course such as ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil. courses...                    

                   

                        It also provide a good industrial training and skill managment. It provides campus interview career paths....
                        The industies such as CISCO, VSLI, etc.. are choosen for industrial trainings
